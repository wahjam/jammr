jammr lets you play with musicians over the internet. It lets you jam together online whenever you want with a community of musicians across all styles and genres. If you've ever gotten bored of backing tracks or practicing alone, jammr makes making music fun again!

Electric, accoustic, software instruments, or vocals

Whether you play an instrument or sing, if there's a way to record on your computer then you can play online using jammr. Get jamming in minutes whether you play guitar, drums, violin, sing, or rap.

Play without lag

jammr just works with almost any internet connection. Lag is not an issue because of the clever way that jammr works. Play in tempo with musicians in Tokyo, LA, and Rio de Janeiro? No problem.

Public and private jam sessions

Public jam sessions are open to anyone and are a fun way to jam with other musicians. But sometimes you want to play more seriously with bandmates or friends. With jammr you can create private jam sessions and only invite your friends, too.

Listen to and share your jams

Jams are automatically recorded so you can listen to or share them with your friends. It's also possible to download individual instrument tracks for remixing.

VST effects and instrument plugins

Use software plugins with jammr to expand your tonal palette. MIDI controllers are also supported for toggling effects or playing notes on software instrument plugins.

Works on Windows, Mac, and Linux

jammr is supported on Windows, Mac, and Linux. It's easy to get started.
