You must not play copyrighted audio that is not compatible with this license over jammr.

Rules of Conduct

jammr is a community of musicians and music fans. The following rules must be observed for all interactions, including jamming, forums, chat, and on the website.

  1. Do not post offensive, obscene, threatening, or illegal content.
  2. Do not post unsolicited advertising.
  3. Do not post malicious content such as viruses, exploits, or trojan horses.
  4. Do not impersonate, mislead, or deceive others.

Failure to follow these rules may lead to termination of your account.

Account and audio data that we store is resilient to some failures but we do not guarantee data will always be available.
