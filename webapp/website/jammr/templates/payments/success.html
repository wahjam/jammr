Private jams

To create a private jam, click "New jam" in the "Connect to server..." dialog. You can control who is allowed to join your private jam session by going to the Admin | Access Control menu item. The friends you have allowed into your jam will see the jam session when they open the "Connect to server..." dialog.

Recorded jams

You will receive notification emails about an hour after you finish a jam so you can listen to your recorded jam sessions. The emails can be disabled on the Edit Profile page.
