jammr is winding down and the software will cease to work on October 31st 2025.

Earlier this year I shut down the company behind jammr and open sourced the platform. Several people from the community offered to help keep jammr going, for which I am grateful. However, we don't have a sustainable path to keep jammr online so it is time to say goodbye.

Luckily there are alternatives where you can jam for free and I think you will find the experience very familiar! I recommend installing JamTaba, a popular online jamming client for Mac, Windows, and Linux that is available standalone or as a plugin. Some of us have been testing JamTaba and it's been a smooth migration.

If you have recorded jams on jammr because you used to be a jammr Premium customer, they will no longer be available after October 31st 2025. You can download them here.

Thank you for being part of jammr and all the best!

Stefan, founder of jammr
